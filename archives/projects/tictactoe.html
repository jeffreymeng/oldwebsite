Tic Tac Toe

Play aganist a computer program. You can't win, but you can try!
You are X. Wins: 0 Loses: 0 Ties: 0 Turn: User
Instructions: If it is your turn, click on any unoccupied slot where you want to place a marker. To win, get three X's in a row. It the computer gets three O's first, you lose. If There are no slots left and no one won it is a tie. If the game is over, the winning row will turn green. Click on any slot to play again.
